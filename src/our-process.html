Design Sprints

The Agile & Lean Way of Designing Digital Products

Design Sprints are the fastest way to find out if a product is worth developing, if a feature is worth the effort, or if your value proposition is really valid. Don’t invest months of time, invest a week.

Step 1 Agree on a problem worth solving
Step 2 Decide on a solution to try
Step 3 Build a prototype to validate if this solution will solve the problem
