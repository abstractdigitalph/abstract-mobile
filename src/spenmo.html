The Challenge

Reinventing Expense Management in South East Asia

Spenmo is a fintech startup backed by Rocket Internet. It’s a spend management platform with smart corporate cards to help businesses gain control and visibility over spending.

It’s is an out-of-the-box business spending solution that offers smart payment cards for employees.

The Process

Creating a roadmap for an MVP

Validating leap of faith assumptions and product ideas

Operating on limited time and runway, the design sprint was a perfect process to quickly identify the core problem which the founders are trying to solve.

Product features are then easily built around these problems and the product roadmap is set.

The Result

A tangible prototype in just 4 days

Prioritizing the right design solutions for the right problems – at the right time.

We validated our product assumptions at the end of the sprint and immediately gained helpful insights on what works and what doesn’t.

Using these newly earned insights, we proceeded to conduct iteration sprints for a freshly polished product.
